Hear me out

Do you have problems with poor acoustics in your room? The acoustical panels are sound dampening and when installing them on walls or the ceiling, they will absorp the sound waves and remove reverberation. Our panels will reach a Class A rating when you install mineral wool behind the panels.

We want to help protecting the environment. That is why our acoustical felt is made from recycled plastic and all the wood we use comes from sustainable forestry. We want to make sure that the forest can reproduce more trees than what we use.

We produce all of our products at our workshop in Ribe, Denmark. Since we produce the product ourselves, we can ensure that the product is top quality and meets our high standards.

Our products are simple and very easy to install. This means that professionals will save a lot of time in the installment process and for private customers it is the perfect ‘Do-It-Yourself’-project.
